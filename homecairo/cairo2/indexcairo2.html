MOEZ STREET
Al-Muizz Lidin Allah Al-Fatimi Street, or the Great Street, or the Kasbah of Cairo, or the Greater Cairo Kasbah, is a street representing the heart of Old Cairo, which was developed to be an open museum of Islamic architecture and antiquities.

KHAN AL-KHALILY
Khan Al-Khalili, one of the neighborhoods of Old Cairo, is a major tourist attraction for visitors to Cairo and Egypt in general. It is characterized by the presence of bazaars, popular shops and restaurants, as well as the large number of tourists and the habituation of its residents to them.

BAB ZUWAILAH
Bab Zuwaila was named after a Berber tribe in North Africa, whose soldiers joined the Jawhar army to conquer Egypt, while the prince of the armies, Badr al-Jamali, ordered its construction in the year 485 AH - 1092.
