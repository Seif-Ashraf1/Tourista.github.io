Do you intend to travel to Marsa Matrouh? Tourism in Marsa Matruh is famous for having the best beaches, as Marsa Matrouh is characterized by its soft white sand, and these beaches attract visitors and tourists.

Gharam Beach
Cleopatra's Baths

Gharam Beach, close to civilization, remains secluded and pristine. It is spacious, the sand is clean and light, without corals, stones and shells - you can safely walk and play with small children. The coasts are decorated with white cliffs. There are no cold currents, the water is warm and calm, and the bottom is gradually gaining depth. The underwater world is picturesque and safe - you can swim even in the evening. Well, you can't do without diving - there are great diving sites on Al-Gharam Beach.
Cleopatra's Baths at Al-Gharam Beach is one of the most important historical and tourist attractions in the city of Marsa Matrouh, which is visited by tourists and visitors to the governorate due to its natural scenery and unique rock formations. Cleopatra's Rock has historical importance as it bears witness to a unique love story between Queen Cleopatra and Antony.

Fayrouz
Beach

The beach is characterized by the purity of its waters, the calmness of its waves, and its soft white sand that enchants the eye. Therefore, children can enjoy the beach without fear of any high waves. In addition, it is distinguished by its cleanliness and the cleanliness of the streets leading to it as well, which attracts many visitors to it.

Agiba
Beach

It is one of Marsa Matrouh's famous tourist attractions, and it is also one of the most beautiful open beaches in Marsa Matrouh, because its waters are gradient colors and it is surrounded by rocks on both sides.
In addition to the above, the beach receives thousands of people from all over the world every year. To see this unique view, explore the hills and rocky caves, and take some pictures for memory.
